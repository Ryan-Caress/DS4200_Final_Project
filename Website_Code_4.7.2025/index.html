Visualizing Historical Stats in Basketball

Our names are Ryan, Luke, and Kyle, and we are passionate about the game of basketball. For our project, we decided to use the statistics provided on Kaggle to analyze different statistics derived from the game. We are hoping to analyze the data through visualizations in Altair, Plotly, and D3 HTML and make some interesting insights. Thanks for joining us on this journey and taking a look at our final project!
